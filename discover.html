OUTDOOR — LAKE VIEW
Outdoor

Where Lake Meets Garden

The villa is surrounded by a breathtakingly landscaped garden, situated just steps from the crystal clear waters of the lake. As a guest, you'll have exclusive access to a private beach where you can take a refreshing swim in the clear and inviting waters.

The outdoor amenities include a hot and cold shower, sink, outdoor furniture with tables and umbrellas, and a spacious parking lot inside the property. There's even a unique sunbathing platform in the middle of the lake.

BEDROOM
Interior

Bedrooms & Living

The house features a spacious living room with kitchen and sofa, plus a fully equipped kitchen with a 4-burner gas stove, dishwasher, refrigerator, kettle, and coffee machine.

Two double bedrooms with comfortable king-size beds and ample wardrobe space. A bathroom with a walk-in shower, WC, bidet, and electric heater rack. Air conditioning for cooling and heating in all rooms.

Accessibility

Designed for Everyone

Villa Volpe is fully accessible for people with disabilities. The property was designed without architectural barriers and has been tested for functionality by people in wheelchairs.

Ramp Entry: A ramp with a 12% gradient from the parking area to the house.

Wheelchair-Friendly Layout: Step-free, with corridors wide enough for wheelchair passage.

Accessible Bathroom: Equipped with handles, a specially designed shower, and a movable seat.

Lakeside Access: On request, we can set up a people-lifting system for lake access.
