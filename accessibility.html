Accessibility

Designed for Everyone

A fully wheelchair accessible holiday home on the sunny shore of Lake Orta — because everyone deserves a lakeside escape.

Barrier-Free Living

Accessible by Design

Villa Volpe has been designed from the ground up to allow full access for people with disabilities. The property was built without architectural barriers and has been tested for functionality by wheelchair users.

We believe that a holiday by the lake should be available to everyone — without compromises on comfort, aesthetics, or experience.

Features

Accessibility Details

Ramp Entry
Access from the parking area to the house via a ramp with a 12% gradient — no steps required at any point.
Wheelchair-Friendly Layout
The entire house is step-free with corridors wide enough for comfortable wheelchair passage throughout all rooms.
Adapted Bathroom
Walk-in shower with grab handles, a movable shower seat, accessible sink height, WC with support bars, bidet, and electric heater rack.
Wide Doorways
All interior doors are wide enough to allow easy wheelchair access, including the bathroom and both bedrooms.
Lakeside Access
On request, we can set up a people-lifting system to allow direct wheelchair access to the lake for swimming and water activities.
Private Parking
A spacious parking area directly connected to the ramp entry — no need to navigate steps or uneven terrain from your car.
