Embracing the Outdoors: Best Orta Lake Outdoor Activities Near Villa Volpe

Hiking the Sacro Monte, cycling around the lake, boat tours to San Giulio, and swimming from your private beach.

Lake Orta's scenic surroundings make it a place that's not just a destination, but a feeling. The Alps and natural beauty around Villa Volpe create a playground for recreation and exploration.

Hiking

The trails accessible from Villa Volpe offer extraordinary experiences, particularly the Sacro Monte di Orta route. Walking through ancient woods, with the songs of birds as your soundtrack, reveals stunning lake vistas at every turn. For those seeking more trails, the "Anello di Ameno: Il Quadrifoglio" offers additional exploration opportunities in the surrounding hills.

Cycling

Rent a bike and explore the lakeside at your own pace. Leisurely rides through villages and alongside the water reveal hidden scenic areas and charming piazzas. For experienced riders, challenging mountain bike routes wind through the surrounding mountains and forests, offering both physical challenge and spectacular views.

Boat Tours

Experience the lake from the water by taking a boat tour to San Giulio island. Villa Volpe's proximity to the water makes it easy to arrange boat excursions that combine adventure with tranquility. The public ferry service runs regularly, or you can arrange private boat tours for a more intimate experience.

Swimming

During summer, the lake's crystal-clear waters invite you for a refreshing swim. Villa Volpe offers direct lake access from the private garden, making it easy to take a dip whenever the mood strikes. The outdoor hot and cold shower is perfect for rinsing off afterward.

Villa Volpe provides the perfect base for outdoor adventures—a comfortable retreat where guests can relax, recharge, and reflect on the day's experiences before heading out for the next adventure.
