About the Property

A Design Glass Cube on Lake Orta

Nestled on the picturesque east shore of Lake Orta, Villa Volpe is a truly unique property. The villa, a design glass cube surrounded by a breathtakingly landscaped garden, is situated just three meters from the crystal clear waters of the lake, with a view of the illuminated island of San Giulio at night.

65 square meters of beautifully appointed living space. Two king-size bedrooms. A fully equipped kitchen. And outside, your private oasis: a lush garden that leads to your own beach.
